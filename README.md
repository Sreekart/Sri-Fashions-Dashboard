📌 Sri Fashion Store Sales & Customer Insights Dashboard
I recently built an interactive dashboard analyzing 3 months of transaction data from a Fashion Store to uncover key business and customer insights.

🔍 Key KPIs Tracked:
• Total Orders
• Total Quantity Sold
• Total Revenue
• Average Customer Rating
• Average Delivery Time

📈 What I Explored:
• 13-week sales and order trends
• Customer buying behavior (how & how much they buy)
• Product popularity and category performance
• Gender distribution of customers
• Geographic distribution of orders
• Delivery performance and shipping timelines
• Customer satisfaction analysis

💡 Key Insights:
• Identified weekly sales patterns and growth trends
• Found majority of customers prefer low-quantity purchases
• Highlighted top-performing products driving revenue
• Observed delivery time impact on customer ratings
• Mapped high-demand regions for better logistics planning

🛠 Tools Used: MS-Excel / Pivot Table / visualizations

This project helped me strengthen my skills in:
✔ Data visualization
✔ Business analysis
✔ Dashboard storytelling
✔ Customer behavior insights
